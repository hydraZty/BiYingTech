Company Info

BiYing Technology

established in early 2018, is a startup company focused on developing innovative applications within the area of blockchain technology.Our flagship BitUniverse app is a self-developed, highly scalable, high-performance blockchain-oriented service node platform. Team members have a strong pedigree from top internet companies including Cheetah Mobile, Tencent, Baidu and Bytedance. BiYing Technology has received over 10 million dollars of investment from Shunwei Capital, Banyan Capital and ZhenFund.

Chen Yong
Founder

Chen Yong joined Kingsoft Corporation in 2001 and oversaw the growth of Duba antivirus, gaining a deep understanding of the security industry and product development in the process. He joined Cheetah Mobile in 2010 as Senior Vice President in charge of the company's mobile flagship products, such as Clean Master and CM Security, which combined have over 1.6 billion global downloads and 600 million MAU.

Olivia Gao
Partner

Olivia graduated from Peking University with a Masters degree of Fine Arts, later joining CCTV as an anchor and journalist gaining media experience for several years. She joined BiYing Technology in charge of PR in Feb 2018.

Mike Zhao
 

Mike joined Kingsoft in 2004 as a researcher in anti-virus technology. He has rich experience in virus analysis, unpacking, and malware detection, and was responsible for designing the Kingsoft Security Cloud 3.0 and FireEye. He joined Cheetah Mobile in 2010 and led development on CM Security, an antivirus app with more than 100 million downloads on Google Play. He now focuses on blockchain research in BiYing Technology.
